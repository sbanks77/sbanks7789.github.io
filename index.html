ABOUT ME

I love technology

As young kid, I would say about 9 or 10, I found excitement in technology, no matter what it was as long as it had something to do with electronics, internet, telephones, etc. I was busy. See i was brought up in the times of dial up internet, beepers, and land line phones. Its crazy because back then the only way we could get on the internet was at night when there was less likely to be alls coming to the house. My mother used to scream from the other side of the house because she knew i had the lines tied up from being on the internet. I would just research, download things, install programs, or just plain old break down a machine and put it back together for the hell of it. As my interest in technology so did my loved for coding and programming. This is my first "Real" site and i hope you guys enjoy. I will be posting new things every week.
